Detailed Description

Demonstrates basic hooking functionality of the ZydisFormatter class and the ability to completely omit specific operands.

This example demonstrates the hooking functionality of the ZydisFormatter class by rewriting the mnemonics of (V)CMPPS and (V)CMPPD to their corresponding alias-forms (based on the condition encoded in the immediate operand).
