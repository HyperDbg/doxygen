EPT_PML2_ENTRY _VMM_EPT_PAGE_TABLE::PML2[VMM_EPT_PML3E_COUNT][VMM_EPT_PML2E_COUNT]

For each 1GB PML3 entry, create 512 2MB entries to map identity. NOTE: We are using 2MB pages as the smallest paging size in our map, so we do not manage individiual 4096 byte pages. Therefore, we do not allocate any PML1 (4096 byte) paging structures.
